How to Play PokéPath TD: Your Complete First-Time Guide
Welcome to [Pokepath TD](https://pokepath.app/)! 
This guide introduces the essential mechanics you need to confidently defend your routes and evolve your Pokémon into elite defenders.

1. Your Mission: The Objective
In Pokepath TD, your goal is to strategically place Pokémon along winding paths to stop enemy waves from reaching the endpoint. Complete all nine routes to prove your dominance in the [Pokepath](https://pokepath.app/games/pokepath) world.

2. Taking Command: The Controls
Controls in Pokepath TD are intuitive and designed for browser-based play using mouse and keyboard.

Action / Purpose	Key(s) / Gesture
Select Pokémon / Place Tower	Left Mouse Click
Move Camera / Scroll Map	Mouse Drag
Zoom In / Out	Mouse Scroll Wheel
Activate / Deactivate Auto Mode	A Key

3. Reading the Battlefield: Your Screen (HUD)
Understanding the HUD is critical for success in Pokepath TD. Monitor wave counters, PokéDollars, Pokémon inventory, and remaining lives to stay ahead of escalating threats.

4. Core Mechanics
In [Pokepath](https://pokepath.app/), defeating enemies earns PokéDollars used for placing and upgrading Pokémon. As Pokémon gain experience, they evolve into stronger forms, unlocking powerful abilities that are essential for surviving later waves.

Strategic team composition is key. Different Pokémon excel in different situations, so experimenting with lineups is a fundamental part of mastering Pokepath TD.

# FAQ
What is PokéPath TD?
[Pokepath TD](https://pokepath.app/) is a Pokémon-themed tower defense game where you defend routes using evolving Pokémon across nine unique paths.

Is progress saved if I lose?
Yes. All Pokémon levels and evolutions in [Pokepath TD](https://pokepath.app/games/pokepath) persist, ensuring every attempt makes you stronger.

Can I play on multiple platforms?
[Pokepath](https://pokepath.app/) TD is playable in browsers (HTML5) and also available on Windows and Linux desktops.

What is Auto Mode?
Auto Mode allows your team to continue battling and farming resources automatically in Pokepath, making AFK progression easy and efficient.

Are Legendary Pokémon included?
Absolutely. Legendary Pokémon represent the ultimate endgame challenge in [Pokepath](https://pokepath.app/games/pokepath) TD and require refined strategies to defeat.
